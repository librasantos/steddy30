Add Calendar Feed

Paste an iCal (.ics) link from any calendar app
How to get your link:
• Google Calendar: Settings → Calendar → Scroll to "Secret address in iCal format"
• TeamSnap: Team → Schedule → Export → iCal link
• GameChanger: Team → gear icon → Schedule Sync → "Sync Schedule to Your Calendar" → copy link (add &1=1 at the end for faster refresh)
• Apple Calendar: Right-click calendar → Share → Copy iCloud link
• Outlook: Settings → View all → Calendar → Shared calendars → Publish
